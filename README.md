# Information Security Policy & Governance Framework

This repository contains a comprehensive information security policy framework developed for a simulated cybersecurity consulting organization (Possis CyberSec, LLC).

The framework demonstrates the application of industry-recognized standards, risk-based security governance, and regulatory compliance principles.

---

## Scope

The policy framework includes:
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Data Classification Policy
- Asset Management Policy
- Social Media Policy
- Business Continuity Policy
- Privacy Policy
- Physical Security Policy

---

## Standards & Frameworks Referenced

- ISO/IEC 27001 & ISO/IEC 27002
- ISO 55000:2014 (Asset Management)
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

---

## Key Concepts Demonstrated

- Risk-based security decision making
- Information security governance
- Asset lifecycle management
- Regulatory and privacy compliance
- Business continuity and resilience
- Policy enforcement and review cycles

---

## Disclaimer

This project was developed as an academic and portfolio exercise.  
It reflects standards alignment and best practices but does not represent formal certification or legal advice.
